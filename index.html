🍰 Day of your Birth Calculator 🎂

This app will help you calculate exactly when will you be having the same day of the week you were born on.

For Example if someone were born on 1/1/1970, Wednesday, the app will calculate when the next time Wednesday will come.

Like 1/1/1980 Wednesday

1/1/2000 Wednesday and so on...

This is a client side javascript app, your data is not being sent anywhere 😊😊😊😊.

Please enjoy and have fun!! 😍



*Only 1945- to the Current Year is a valid entry

*Calculations is only for the next 80 years
